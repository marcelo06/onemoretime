One More Time

We are a Rock band from Colombia formed en 2017.

We have a big influence in the classic rock bands like Aerosmith, Guns n Roses, Led zeppelin and Queen.

Line up 2017

GUSTAVO SANCHEZ / Bass

CRISTIAN MUÑOZ / Guitar

SEBASTIAN CESPEDES / Drums

Story

Everything began in the NORMAL SUPERIOR OF THE QUINDIO, when music teacher Gustavo Sanchez decided to form a representative musical group called "SIN BATERIA", which would change its name 2 years later by "ROCK 15".

There he met Sebastian Cespedes and Cristian Muñoz, who got along very well because of their musical tastes.

Gustavo and Sebastian met occasionally in the house of some of them to play themes of Iron Maiden or Kraken, while Gustavo and Cristian continued in Rock 15.

Finally Gustavo and Sebastian joined a project of a friend of theirs named Daniel Solis, who later formed a group called Danan.

In 2013, Cristian Muñoz and Miguel Muñoz - a friend who had played in Rock 15 - would join Danan to give him the necessary strength at that time.

After 2 years the leader, vocalist and founder of Danan leaves the city, finishing the project. After the departure of Daniel in 2015, Miguel Muñoz's girlfriend, Alejandra Correa joins the group as a vocalist. From that moment they were called 2nd Time which would disintegrate in 2016.

They briefly formed a band called Suite, who had Juan Abadia in the voice and Jimena Agudelo on the keyboard
